A Thousand Splendid Suns

by Khaled Hosseini

It's a "hard to put down" book.

Khaled Hosseini's writing transports you directly into the heart of Afghanistan, He writes so beautifully and vividly that every character feels utterly real. This book is just immaculate (it moved me deeply). A highly recommended book by me. I believe that everyone should read it atleast once in their lives. A five star book for me. Spoilers: This book will rip your heart into million pieces scatter it across the whole universe.

A man's heart is a wretched, wretched thing. It isn't like a mother's womb. It won't bleed. It won't stretch to make room for you.

Behind every trial and sorrow that He makes us shoulder, God has a reason.

One could not count the moons that shimmer on her roofs, Or the thousand splendid suns that hide behind her walls.
